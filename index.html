About Me

A technology professional with a long background in IT and telecommunications, shaped by international and multicultural environments. A strong sense of responsibility and commitment has been a consistent part of the journey — both towards the teams worked with and the customers supported. Focus has always been placed on delivering reliable results, solving problems effectively, and contributing positively to team goals. Curiosity and continuous learning have played an important role throughout the career, from software engineering and cartography to services, support, and integration engineering roles in complex telecom environments.

Over the years, experience has been built not only in technical systems, but also in collaboration, communication, mentoring, and working in cross-functional teams. Recently, the focus has expanded toward data analytics, Python, and machine learning through formal studies in Helsinki, opening a new direction that combines technical experience with data-driven thinking. A mindset built on responsibility, curiosity, and continuous improvement continues to guide the search for roles where both technical and collaborative skills can contribute to meaningful outcomes.
